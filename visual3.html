New Covid Cases vs Sanitation Standards

How It Works:

  • Year Selection: Choose between 2020, 2021, and 2022 to see the change in COVID-19 cases over time.
  • Sanitation Standards: Adjust the sanitation level to see the impact on COVID-19 cases. The levels are:
    • High: Most people have access to proper sanitation like soap and public bathrooms.
    • Medium: Limited access to sanitation facilities.
    • Low: Poor access to sanitation, with some areas having public defecation.
  • Color Coding: Countries are color-coded based on new COVID-19 cases:
    • Red: High number of new cases
    • Orange: Moderate to high number of new cases
    • Yellow: Moderate number of new cases
    • Green: Low number of new cases
    • Grey: No data available
